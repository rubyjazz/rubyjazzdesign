Project Name: Net Neutrality
What I Did: Design & assets
Animation: Kim Estoesta, James Kang



Net Neutrality

As part of a very quick (about 4 hours turnaround) project, my coworkers and I created a set of graphics to promote net neutrality after the FCC annoucement to strike it down.

I created the assets and explored color and style options while my two teammates provided feedback and created the animation. Although it's never relaxing to work extremely fast on projects, this was something that all three of us were passionate enough about to put aside our other responsibilities and hash it out. I absolutely love working with other people who believe in what they are doing and this project particularly taught me the ins and outs of working with other people under pressure.
